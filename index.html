Panther Tips

Welcome to the JLS Panther Pals website! This website is a collection of helpful information for incoming 6th graders. It includes useful information about middle school and will help ease the transition from elementary school to middle school for incoming panthers. Also included in this website are helpful links that will go provide in-depth information about particular topics. For any questions regarding middle school, be sure to check the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) page. If there are any unanswered questions, email the principal (Sharon Ofek) at sofek@pausd.org, contact the main office at 650-856-5188, or visit the JLS website at jls.pausd.org for more details.
